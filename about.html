About Us

Asset Health Monitoring: SHMS & EEWS Solutions for Real-Time Monitoring

As assets such as buildings, bridges, piers and civil structures age, they degrade. Damage such as cracks can appear unnoticed and worsen over time. By the time damage is detected using conventional visual inspection and non-destructive testing methods, it is too late to prevent asset failure.

We offer sensor-based SHMS & EEWS solutions that provide continuous, real-time monitoring of the health of your critical assets. This allows you to identify and respond to failures before they seriously impact your operations or finances.

Our sensor-based solution is like having a Technician on site 24/7 without the added cost of human intervention. It uses the Internet of Things (IoT) to continuously assess damage based on NDT standards. By enabling you to respond to damage before it becomes a disaster, our solution reduces the risk of asset failure. This allows for scheduling preventative maintenance and helps you in operational and financial decision making.
