Our Tools

We provide a variety of tools designed for advanced asset monitoring to ensure safety and reliability in infrastructure management.

Accelerometer

Measures acceleration forces that may be acting on an object. Useful for detecting vibrations and movements.

Strain Gauge

Measures the amount of strain or deformation in an object. Essential for monitoring structural health.

Tilt Meter

Measures the tilt or inclination of an object. Useful for detecting shifts in structural alignment.

Displacement Sensor

Measures the movement of an object from a reference point. Ideal for monitoring shifts in position.

Environmental Sensor

Monitors environmental conditions such as temperature and humidity. Essential for comprehensive asset monitoring.

Other Sensor

Measures the temperature of an environment or object. Useful for thermal monitoring in various applications.
