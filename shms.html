Structure Health Monitoring System (SHMS)

STRUCTURE HEALTH MONITORING SYSTEM

A structure health monitoring (SHM) system is a system that continuously monitors structures like buildings and bridges to assess their integrity and detect damage.

BRIDGES

BRIDGE MONITORING | STRUCTURAL HEALTH | STRESS ANALYSIS | SAFETY ALERTS | DINAMIC DISPLACEMENT

Our SHMS is designed to monitor the health of bridges in real time, providing critical insights into structural integrity and potential wear. Timely alerts enable preventive maintenance and ensure long-term safety, helping prevent catastrophic failures and prolonging infrastructure lifespan.

BUILDINGS

SKYSCRAPERS | HOSPITALS | OFFICES | MALLS | RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS

Our SHMS monitors critical infrastructures, from skyscrapers to hospitals, offering real-time data on structural health. This enables maintenance teams to make informed decisions to prevent costly repairs and improve safety for occupants.

DAM

HIGHWAY MONITORING | PAVEMENT HEALTH | WEAR ANALYSIS | LOAD STRESS | SAFETY MEASURES

SHMS provides continuous monitoring of roads and highways, assessing wear and structural performance. By detecting early signs of stress or degradation, it enables timely maintenance, preventing accidents and reducing downtime.
