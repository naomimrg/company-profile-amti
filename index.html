Asset monitoring refers to the use of technology to continuously track and manage assets in real-time to ensure their proper functioning and timely maintenance.

The monitoring system uses sensors and data collection tools to gather information about asset conditions. This data is then transmitted to a central system where it is analyzed and used to trigger alerts or maintenance actions.

Asset monitoring provides real-time insights into asset performance, helps in early detection of potential issues, reduces maintenance costs, and enhances the overall efficiency and lifespan of assets.
