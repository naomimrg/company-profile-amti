Why Dam Monitoring?

Dams are critical infrastructures for water storage, energy generation, and flood control. Monitoring their structural integrity is essential to prevent failures that could lead to catastrophic consequences. Our dam monitoring solutions provide real-time data on structural behavior, environmental factors, and water levels, allowing for early detection of potential issues and timely maintenance.

Key Features

Water Level Monitoring

Our system continuously monitors water levels in reservoirs, ensuring early warning for potential overflows or dam failures due to high water pressure.

Structural Health Monitoring

Sensors embedded within the dam structure measure deformation, cracks, and stresses, providing real-time insights into the dam's integrity.
