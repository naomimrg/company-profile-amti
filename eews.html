Earthquake Early Warning System (EEWS)

Earthquake Early Warning System (EEWS) is an innovative safety technology designed to detect seismic waves in real time. Unlike predicting earthquakes, EEWS utilizes advanced sensors and AI to rapidly assess the magnitude and intensity of seismic events before they reach you.

Get In Touch

System-level Plant-wide Performance

By identifying P-waves, the first waves to arrive in an earthquake, our system provides crucial alerts seconds to minutes before the stronger S-waves hit. This early notification allows users to take swift actions, such as moving to a safer location or halting critical operations, ultimately saving lives and minimizing damage.

  • Real-time Detection: Sensors placed strategically to detect seismic activity instantly.
  • AI Integration: Rapid assessment of earthquake magnitude and its potential impact.
  • Instant Alerts: Sends notifications to your devices, offering precious seconds to act.
  • Safety First: Designed to protect individuals, businesses, and infrastructure.
